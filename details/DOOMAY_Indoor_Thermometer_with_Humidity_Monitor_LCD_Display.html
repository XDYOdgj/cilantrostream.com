DOOMAY Indoor Thermometer and Hygrometer

Stay informed about your indoor environment with this reliable temperature and humidity monitor. Designed for accuracy, convenience, and ease of use, it's the perfect addition to any room in your home.

Clear and Easy-to-Read Display

This DOOMAY thermometer features a large, high-visibility LCD display that makes it simple to check temperature and humidity from any angle or distance. Whether standing by the bed, sitting at your desk, or halfway across the room, the numbers are always visible and easy to interpret.

Precision and Real-Time Updates

Equipped with a high-accuracy sensor, this device delivers precise temperature and humidity readings, ensuring you have trustworthy data to maintain optimal indoor conditions. With a refresh interval of every 10 seconds, you'll always be in the know and able to adjust your environment accordingly.

Comfort Indicator with Face Icons

The comfort indicator provides an intuitive way to assess your indoor air quality. With easy-to-recognize DRY, COMFORT, and WET icons, you can quickly determine if you need to run a humidifier or dehumidifier to achieve the perfect balance.

User-Friendly Interface

Designed with simplicity in mind, this indoor thermometer allows you to switch between Fahrenheit and Celsius with a single press. It also features a 12h/24h time format toggle, an integrated date display, and a real-time clock, making it a versatile and functional tool for daily life.

Durable and Portable Design

Whether you need it in your kitchen, baby room, office, or living area, the compact and lightweight build of this meter makes it easy to place or move as needed. It also includes multiple mounting options such as a desktop stand or wall adhesive, giving you flexibility in how you use it.

Long-Lasting Battery Life

The DOOMAY thermometer is powered by a durable CR2450 battery, offering extended use without the hassle of frequent replacements. You can monitor your environment day after day with minimal maintenance.

Whether you're tracking weather in your home, monitoring conditions for your baby, or ensuring comfort in your workspace, the DOOMAY indoor thermometer and hygrometer is built to provide accurate, real-time information with ease.
